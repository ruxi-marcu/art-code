Image Processing

Shows some examples of different operations made on images.
Some are applied on individual pixels like: color inversion, grey scale and different transformations (mirror, upside down) and
some are on groups of pixels using convolution with a kernel: edge-detection, emboss, blur and sharpen.

Face Detection

Turns on the camera and detects if there is a face and displays under it the emotion displayed: neutral, happy, sad, angry etc.

Green Screen

Replaces the green screen in a video with a different video.
